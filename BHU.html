General Information

General information about Banaras Hindu University(BHU) 

NIRF Ranking

BHU stands at the 3rd position in the NIRF Rankings of 2022 in the University Category. They have a 3.41 CGPA under NAAC Accredition.
NIRF Ranking :NIRF Ranking

Placements

 According to BHU NIRF report 2022, a total of 431 students were placed out of 3,456 students during Banaras Hindu University UG (3-year) placements 2021. Further, the median package offered during BHU UG (3-year) placements 2021 was INR 7 LPA.
Placements: BHU Placements 

CutOffs

BHU has a fixed qualifying cutoff for both UET and PET. Each of the applicants needs to score more than the minimum cutoff criteria to qualify the respective entrance exams .
Cutoffs: BHU cutoffs
