About OU
Osmania University is a state funded university which is in the heart of Hyderabad Metropolis and receives endowments from Govt of Telangana

READ MORE

Vision of the University

The Vision of the University is to generate and disseminate knowledge through a harmonious blend of ancient and modern wisdom, and to serve the society by developing in students heightened intellectual, cultural, ethical, and humane sensitivities; to foster a scientific temper, and to promote professional and technological expertise.
